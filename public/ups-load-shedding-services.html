About Service

An uninterruptible power supply (UPS), also known
as a battery backup, provides backup power when
your regular power source fails or voltage drops to an unacceptable level. A UPS allows for the safe,
orderly shutdown of a computer and connected
equipment. We recommend, install and maintain UPS systems for your household, commercial and industrial electrical needs.
